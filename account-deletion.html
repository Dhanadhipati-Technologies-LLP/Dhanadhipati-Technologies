Account Deletion Request

If you wish to permanently delete your account or personal data associated with Ludo Wordo, please send an email to:

Email: cs.dhanadhipatitechnologies@gmail.com

Include the following details:

  • Registered username or user ID
  • Registered email address
  • Subject line: Account Deletion Request

What Happens Next

  • Verification of your identity
  • Account deletion within 7 business days
  • Confirmation email sent after deletion
Important:
  • Deleted accounts cannot be recovered
  • If you used Google or Facebook login, send the request from the same registered email
  • Some financial or legal records may be retained as required by law
