Account Deletion Request

If you wish to permanently delete your account or any personal data associated with Ludo Wordo, please send an email to:

Email: cs.dhanadhipatitechnologies@gmail.com

Please include the following details in your request:

  • Registered username or user ID
  • Registered email address
  • Subject line: Account Deletion Request

What Happens Next:

  • Our team will verify your identity to protect your data privacy.
  • Once verified, your account and associated data will be permanently deleted within 7 business days.
  • You will receive a confirmation email once the deletion is completed.

Important:

  • After deletion, you will not be able to recover your account, data, or game progress.
  • If you signed in using Google, Facebook, or other linked accounts, please send the request using the same email or contact method.
  • Some financial or transactional data may be retained temporarily for legal or accounting purposes.
  • If you reinstall or register again, a new account will be created.
