Ludo Wordo

Ludo Wordo is an educational, word-based game that combines classic Ludo-style gameplay with strategic vocabulary learning.

What Is Ludo Wordo?

Ludo Wordo turns a familiar board-game experience into something smarter. Players move pawns across a board filled with alphabets, collect the right letters, and complete a single target word to win.

It’s not about memorizing words. It’s about **thinking ahead, planning moves, and outplaying opponents**.

The Core Objective

  • Each match presents one target word
  • Some alphabets are missing
  • Players collect the missing alphabets on the board
  • The first player to complete the word wins

How the Game Is Played

  • Pawns move based on dice rolls
  • The movement path is filled with alphabets
  • Pawns keep moving until the word is completed
  • Killing an opponent pawn allows collecting one alphabet from them
  • Meaning and pronunciation clues help when needed

Multiplayer & Rewards

  • Real-time online multiplayer
  • Private game rooms with friends
  • 2-player and 4-player matches
  • Entry fees using coins
  • Rewards from spinning the wheel and daily rewards
  • Magic boxes with surprise gifts

Progress & Community

  • Global leaderboards with weekly, monthly, and seasonal rankings
  • House of Words to revisit words from winning games
  • Friends and referral features to grow your circle
