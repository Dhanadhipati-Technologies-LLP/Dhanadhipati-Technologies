Ludo Wordo

Ludo Wordo is an educational word-based game that combines classic Ludo gameplay with vocabulary learning, strategy, and competition.

What Exactly Is Ludo Wordo?

Ludo Wordo is an innovative, strategy-based board game where players collect alphabets, move pawns thoughtfully, and complete a target word to win.

It is not about racing. It is not about memorization. It is about smart movement, sharp decisions, and outplaying opponents.

Every move can give you an alphabet.
Every alphabet can open new possibilities.
The right word ends the game.

The Core Objective

  • Each match begins with one target word
  • Some alphabets of the word are missing
  • Players must identify and collect the missing alphabets
  • The first player to complete the word wins instantly

How the Gameplay Works

Pawns and Movement

  • Players move pawns based on the number shown on dice rolls
  • The entire movement path is filled with alphabets
  • There is no finish point — the game continues until the word is completed

Collecting Alphabets

  • Landing on a cell allows the player to collect that alphabet
  • Collected alphabets are used to complete the target word
  • Strategic movement is key to winning faster

Pawn Battles

  • If a player kills an opponent’s pawn
  • The attacking player can collect one alphabet from the opponent
  • This adds tactical depth and direct competition

Clues

  • Meaning-based clues help players identify the word
  • Pronunciation-based clues are available when needed

Multiplayer & Game Modes

  • Real-time multiplayer with players online
  • Create private game rooms and invite friends
  • Play 2-player or 4-player matches

Rewards & In-Game Systems

Coins & Entry Fees

Each match requires an entry fee paid using coins. Winning games rewards players with more coins.

Wheel & Daily Rewards

Players can earn rewards by spinning the wheel and by claiming daily rewards for regular play.

Magic Boxes

Magic boxes contain surprise gifts. Unboxing them may unlock special cards that help players access additional clues during gameplay.

Leaderboards & Progress

  • Global leaderboards
  • Weekly, monthly, and seasonal rankings
  • Exciting prizes for top-performing players

House of Words

Every time a player wins a match, the word from that game is added to their House of Words.

Players can revisit these words anytime and learn from their victories.

Friends & Referrals

  • Send and accept friend requests
  • Invite friends using the referral feature
