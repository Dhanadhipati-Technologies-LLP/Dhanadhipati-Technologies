Ludo Wordo

Ludo Wordo is an educational word-based game that combines classic Ludo gameplay with vocabulary learning, strategy, and competition.

What Is Ludo Wordo?

Ludo Wordo is an innovative, strategy-based board game where players collect alphabets, move pawns thoughtfully, and complete a given word to win.

It is not about racing.
It is not about memorization.
It is about smart movement, sharp decisions, and outplaying opponents.

  • Every move can give you an alphabet
  • Every alphabet can bring you closer to winning the game
  • The right word ends the game

The Core Objective

  • Each match begins with one given word
  • Some alphabets of the word are missing
  • Players must identify and collect the missing alphabets
  • The first player to complete the word wins instantly

How the Gameplay Works

Pawns and Movement

  • Players move pawns based on the number shown on dice rolls
  • The entire Ludo game path is filled with alphabets
  • There is no finish point — the game continues until the word is completed

Collecting Alphabets

  • Landing on a cell allows the player to collect that alphabet
  • Collected alphabets are used to complete the given word
  • Strategic movement decides how fast you win

Pawn Battles

  • Killing an opponent’s pawn allows alphabet stealing
  • One alphabet can be collected from the opponent
  • This adds risk, aggression, and tactical depth

Clues

  • Meaning-based clues help identify the word
  • Pronunciation-based clues assist when stuck

Online Multiplayer & Play with Friends

  • Real-time online multiplayer with players across the world
  • Create a private game room and invite friends
  • Join games using a room code
  • Supports both 2-player and 4-player matches

Coins & Entry Fee System

  • Each match requires an entry fee paid using coins
  • Winning games rewards players with coins
  • Coins act as the core in-game currency

Wheel & Daily Rewards

  • Players can earn rewards by spinning the wheel
  • Daily rewards encourage consistent play
  • These rewards help players continue playing even if coins run out

Friends & Referrals

  • Add friends by searching and entering their username
  • Send and accept friend requests
  • Refer friends to join Ludo Wordo
  • Playing with friends makes the experience more competitive and fun

House of Words

  • Every winning word is stored automatically
  • Players build a personal word collection
  • Words can be revisited and learned anytime
  • Progress stays even after the match ends
