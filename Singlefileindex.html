Ludo Wordo — Play Ludo. Gain Wordo.

Where letters become your weapons, words become your victories, and learning becomes your superpower.

The Problem We’re Solving

Language learning today feels like a chore — repetitive, dull, and disconnected from real life. Millions of learners — especially students, illiterates, and competitive exam aspirants — struggle to build spelling, vocabulary, and pronunciation skills because traditional methods fail to engage and motivate them.

Our Solution — Learning That Feels Like Play

Ludo Wordo transforms the classic Ludo board into a language battlefield where every dice roll leads to learning. Players are given words with missing alphabets, and as they move their tokens across the board, they collect letters, complete words, and build vocabulary — all while playing a thrilling strategy game.

How to Play

  1. Join a Room: Choose between 2-player or 4-player mode.
  2. Get Your Word: Each player receives a word with missing letters.
  3. Roll the Dice: Move your tokens strategically to land on the tiles showing the letters you need.
  4. Collect & Complete: Gather the correct alphabets to finish your word first.
  5. Win Rewards: Earn coins, trophies, and word power — your “House of Words” grows stronger with every victory!

Key Features

  • Real-Time Multiplayer

  • Coin & Reward System
  • Educational Word Packs
  • Daily rewards and Spin wheel
